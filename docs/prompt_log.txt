PROJECT_NAME: Toshiba TMPM4KNF10AFG Micromouse
MCU: TMPM4KNF10AFG (ARM Cortex-M4, 160 MHz, 5V)
IDE: Keil µVision
DEBUGGER: CMSIS-DAP with bidirectional level shifter

HARDWARE:
- Motors: TB67H450AFNG + Pololu #5211 N20 30:1 with 12 CPR side‑connector encoder
- Encoder: A-ENC32 (on-chip, quadrature, 4× edge counting)
- IR sensors: 4 pairs (far left, left, right, far right) with ADC + DMA
- PWM: T32A ch0 (left) and ch3 (right) at 40 kHz
- Control tick: 1 kHz (T32A1 interval timer)

MODULES (existing and planned):

  - adc.c/h, dma.c/h, gpio.c/h, timer32A.c/h, encoder32A.c/h, systick.c/h

  - Motor.c/h          (PWM + direction)
  - IrSensor.c/h       (IR sampling + distance)
  - Encoder.c/h        (speed/position from encoders)
  - PID.c/h            (PID controller math)
  - Motion.c/h         (speed control, PID + motor)
  - Maze.c/h           (maze state, wall map)
  - FloodFill.c/h      (flood fill algorithm)

# KNOWN CONSTANTS (for quick reference)
# ----------------------------------------------------------------------------
ENCODER:
  - Motor shaft: 12 counts per revolution (CPR) – already quadrature‑decoded.
  - Gearbox: 30:1 nominal (exact 29.89:1).
  - Wheel CPR: 12 × 29.89 ≈ 359 → use 360 for simplicity.
  - 1 wheel revolution = 360 counts.
  - Speed in CPS = (delta over 1 ms) × 1000.

MOTOR PWM:
  - Timer: T32A ch0 (left) and ch3 (right).
  - Frequency: 40 kHz (period = 2000 counts @ 80 MHz).
  - Duty: 0–100% → compare register values 0..1999.

CONTROL LOOP:
  - Tick: 1 kHz (T32A1 interval timer, period = 80000 counts @ 80 MHz).
  - dt = 0.001 sec.
